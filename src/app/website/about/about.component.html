Hello, I'm Mario García

UUX/UI designer based in Mexico

I firmly believe that a great digital experience can transform the way people connect with brands, and the strong impact it has on business.

I've been working in experience and interface design for digital products for over eight years, collaborating with creative teams and international brands such as M&M's®, Lucas®, Snickers®, Ferrara®, CoverGirl®, among others. I like to think that my work not only solves problems but also creates memorable moments for users.

I believe that design doesn't begin on the screen, but in life itself. Every experience, every conversation, and every walk outdoors can be the start of a great idea.

Outside of the digital world, I love everything related to art. In my free time, I paint with oils, a passion that allows me to disconnect, explore new ideas, and keep my creativity alive. I also enjoy skating in the park, changing the pace, clearing my mind, and finding inspiration in everyday life.
