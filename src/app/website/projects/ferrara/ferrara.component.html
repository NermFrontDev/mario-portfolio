Ferrara - Inspiring Sweetness

UX/UI DESIGN | WEBSITE DESIGN

Ferrara is a brand that has stretched the sugary limits of imagination for over 115 years to create confections that brings people joy. The belief that candy brings people closer has made Ferrara the undisputed leader in sugar confections in the US.

Has grown from a humble sweets shop into the creators of some of the worlds most iconic candy brands.

The Problem

We were tasked with transforming Ferrara's outdated and static website; it wasn't aligned with what the brand stood for. It needed a strong digital presence that reflected its leadership, innovation, and strong connection with its entire community.

The Challenge

The challenge was to transform the entire website, with a fresh and striking design proposal, taking into account important points such as:

  • Story about the heritage of Ferrara: this should be the enterpiece that would highlight Ferrara's legacy.
  • The strong connection that ferrara has with the community: Highlight the culture and how Ferrara impacts the community through its values.
  • Innovation: the passion for the perfecting the art of sugar and constant exploration of possibilities.

    • Design Process

    As a team had some meetings to map the entire site, we knew we would have a lot of pages on the site and needed to clarify the flow of the site so we wouldn't miss important details in the future.

    Exploring in the wireframes

    I started creating wireframes from sections and pages to begin identify the graphic style and general structure of the site.

    This was a stage of exploration and very creative, full of feedback that nourished and made the first part of the project stronger and more creative.

    This first phase helped me to give a structural proposal of the site to the client. I already had a starting point and with early feedback from the client, I understood more deeply the way the client thought and what he expected in the future.

    Start Building Components

    From the beginning I knew there was great potential opportunity to create a visually striking and colorful UI that would reflect Ferrara's legacy and all of his values throughout the website, I started creating some visual concepts with the team.

    It was a team process, very exciting, and full of creative exploration, the concept of having sweets floating throughout the website gave us the opportunity to build a visual identity with a lot of potential in the different sections of the site.

    Many of these components were intended to be animations, but at the moment we were discovering the ideas that worked, in which we would touch base to fine-tune many details, but we were really liking what we were getting to.

    bringing to life the heart of the Website

    We worked as a team to resolve the central point of the page. Working on the creative proposal was challenging, fun, and involved a lot of creative exploration. Understanding the full story from the beginning was essential to landing better visual proposals.

    Key points to include:

  • Identity and legacy: Show a history of more than a century and a half, from a small family shop to a powerful global corporation, highlighting its ability to evolve without losing its roots.
  • Sustained innovation: Illustrate how Ferrara has continually reinvented itself (new techniques, new products, strategic acquisitions), allowing it to remain relevant in a dynamic market.
  • Strategic expansion: Highlight key moments of growth: mergers, acquisitions, and industrial expansion, which have strengthened its presence in the US, Brazil, and other international markets.
  • Emotional narrative: Telling a story built with personal elements (such as Lemonhead) and impactful events (such as its appearance in the Super Bowl) helps create an emotional connection between the brand and the public.
  • Corporate transparency: Telling your corporate story clearly and chronologically, including how Ferrara communicates its values, vision for the future, and commitment to innovation, as well as how it strengthens its image among consumers, employees, and investors.

    • We included an interactive history of Ferrara's heritage. This page was animated as a centerpiece that would highlight Ferrara's legacy.

    The full spectrum of sweetness

    A large portfolio brands as diverse as they are delicious.

    Ferrara brings together a wide variety of popular brands, from American classics to Brazilian labels, backed by impressive production figures. The idea was to invites the user to explore its entire portfolio, brand by brand, and learn more about its history and purpose.

    Many of these components were intended to be animations, but at the moment we were discovering the ideas that worked, in which we would touch base to fine-tune many details, but we were really liking what we were getting to.

    Responsive Web Design

    Once we had approved navigation screens and sections of entire pages, I started translating everything to a mobile version.

  • Prioritizing Visual Content: I put the focuses on large images, vibrant colors, and visual cards for each brand (Brach's®, Black Forest®, Jelly Belly®), ideal for quick and engaging touch navigation.
  • Clear and consistent calls to action: Learn More buttons allow direct navigation to each section or brand story, facilitating intuitive exploration on small screens.
  • Built-in Accessibility: incorporate an alt text on images, keyboard navigation, good color contrast, resizable text, and form labels, with the goal of achieving good accessibility on mobile devices.
